REAL WORLD
AI outside the tech bubble.
AI adoption looks completely different for a print shop vs. a tech startup
most AI tools get abandoned after the first week in non tech businesses
the gap between what demos promise and what operators need is massive
onboarding non technical users into agentic workflows is the real challenge
the simplest use case that delivers the most value wins every time

ENGINEER TO MARKETER

THE SHIFT
marketing is the new moat.
AI is commoditizing engineering, making human psychology and persuasion the edge
engineers are historically terrible at marketing, training that muscle is urgent
CS programs in 2026 should arguably be 50/50 technical and marketing
the engineer with both technical and marketing fluency is the most valuable in tech
