YC request for startups — AI-native ideas startups
Product & decision AI: Cursor for Product Managers — AI that figures out WHAT to build, not just how. Synthesizes customer feedback into feature decisions. AI for Government — agencies get AI-generated applications but process them by hand, massive bottleneck.

Finance & stablecoins: AI-Native Hedge Funds — entirely new strategies only possible with agents analyzing filings and executing trades. Stablecoin Financial Services — yield accounts, cross-border payments, investment access on stablecoin rails. Connects to Stripe/Tempo and Whop announcements this week.

Services & physical work: AI-Native Agencies — design, legal, video, marketing where AI does the work and humans do the taste. Software margins on a services business. AI Guidance for Physical Work — real-time coaching via cameras and wearables for field workers. Skilled labor shortage + AI = huge gap.
link
Whop — AI businesses making money right now revenue
Teaching AI business: 6-Figure AI Agency Launchpad ($18.6K/mo, 397 members) and AI Cashflow ($12K/mo, 240 members) — combined $30K+/mo just teaching people to build AI businesses.

Trading & prompts: AutoGPT Trading Signals ($8.5K/mo, 170 members) and Prompt Vault ($6.2K/mo, 310 members) — automated signals and curated prompt libraries generating $14.7K/mo combined.

Content systems: AI Content Factory ($4.8K/mo, 95 members) — AI video and content generation systems. Real recurring revenue businesses built entirely on AI workflows.

⠿ 02 the tool shed
~10 min
Google Stitch overhaul tool
Google overhauled Stitch with a context-aware design agent, voice-driven design mode, instant interactive prototyping, and a DESIGN.md system for enforcing design consistency across screens. This is design tooling meeting agentic AI head-on.
NemoClaw — Nvidia's enterprise agent wrapper tool
Jensen Huang announced NemoClaw at GTC. A security/privacy wrapper on top of OpenClaw. Adds guardrails via OpenShell, policy enforcement for how agents handle data, and optional local model inference via Nemotron so sensitive workloads stay off the cloud. Same autonomous agent framework underneath, just hardened for enterprise.
Agents everywhere — 4 new agent platforms tools
Anthropic remote task dispatch: Launched in Cowork — assign tasks to Claude from your phone or desktop in one persistent conversation.

Stride Health Nora: AI-powered health insurance guide matching users to HealthCare.gov plans by estimating tax credits, checking doctor/medication coverage, and recommending best fit.

MuleRun: Always-on AI agent running on a dedicated 24/7 computer, self-evolves via collective intelligence from real business workflows, proactively monitors and acts on your behalf.

Manus desktop app: Brings its cloud AI agent onto your local machine — executing CLI commands to manage files, build apps, and automate workflows using your local tools, GPU, and applications, with explicit approval required for every action.

⠿ 03 agentic payments
~10 min
Stripe + Tempo — agent payment standard payments
Stripe and Tempo have launched an open standard allowing AI agents to transact autonomously using stablecoins, cards, and BNPL. This is the first serious infrastructure play for agents that need to spend money on their own.
link
Whop payments network — open to all devs payments
Whop opened its payments network to all developers. Supports global payouts (ACH, crypto, Venmo, PayPal), payments (cards, Apple Pay, crypto, BNPL), balance yield up to 6% APY, and embedded chat. This is the full-stack payments layer that agent-powered businesses will run on.
link
⠿ 04 zero-human companies
~15 min
Durable — full business in minutes platform
Platform that launches a full business in minutes by generating a website, brand, SEO, domain, CRM, payments, revenue plan, and AI agents for marketing, discoverability (e.g. ranking on ChatGPT/Google), and competitor research. The one-click company.
Polsia — AI runs every department platform
Platform that launches and runs entire companies. Handles engineering, marketing, support, ops, and more. Not just a launcher — it keeps operating the company after creation.
Paperclip — open-source agent org chart framework
Open-source orchestration scheme that provides org charts, goal alignment, task ownership, budgets, governance, agent templates, heartbeats for 24/7 operation, audit logs, and support for any AI agents (OpenClaw, Claude Code, Codex) to function as a structured team under roles like CEO/CTO/engineers/marketers.
FelixCraft — the autonomous AI CEO wild
Autonomous AI agent built on OpenClaw by Nat Eliason. Runs as the CEO of its own company, builds and sells AI tools/products like playbooks and the Claw Mart marketplace for agent skills, generates revenue (hundreds of thousands reported), manages its own finances/ops, and posts independently on X. No human in the loop.
